Bio
Family! I’m glad you're here. Let's get to know eachother a bit better :)
JC Kalinks, born and raised in Zambia, is a talented musician celebrated for his unique fusion of Afrobeat, dancehall, and hip-hop. With a passion for storytelling and a knack for creating infectious beats, he uses music as a platform to express life’s struggles, triumphs, and everyday experiences. Known as "The Bluetooth," JC Kalinks is not just a musician but a voice for his generation, connecting fans through relatable lyrics and memorable performances. His hit tracks, such as "Maradona" and "Ichibyongo," have earned him recognition both locally and internationally. JC Kalinks remains dedicated to inspiring and entertaining through music that speaks to the soul.
